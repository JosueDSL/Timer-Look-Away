Timer Look Away!


Is a simple timer that will alert you when it's time to look away from your computer screen.

It's based on the 20-20-20 rule, which states that every 20 minutes, you should look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

TimerLookAway! will alert you every 20 minutes with a sound and a popup message.
